Right where you need it

PocketLink doesn't live in another tab or a clunky popup. It's built directly into your browser's native right-click menu, making it feel like a natural part of your workflow from day one.

INSTANT

Click, copied, done.

The moment your shortlink is generated, it's automatically and silently copied to your clipboard. There's no extra step. Just click, and you're ready to paste your link anywhere.

Easy setup in minutes

1

Add to your browser

Install the extension from the official Chrome Web Store. It's free, secure, and takes just one click.

2

Get your token

Log in to your Bit.ly account and generate a new generic access token from the API settings.

3

Save your token

Click the PocketLink icon in your browser toolbar, paste your token into the settings page, and click save. You're all set!
